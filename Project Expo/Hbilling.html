Welcome

Hotel Deluna

The hotel's attention to detail extends to its event spaces, providing a sophisticated backdrop for weddings, conferences, and special occasions. Cutting-edge technology seamlessly integrates with timeless elegance, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience for all guests.

Every detail, from the tasteful artwork to the plush furnishings, reflects a commitment to elegance and comfort. The rooms and suites are meticulously designed, featuring sumptuous linens, state-of-the-art amenities, and breathtaking views of the surrounding cityscape. Guests are pampered with personalized services, including a dedicated concierge and 24-hour room service.

Culinary experiences at the hotel are nothing short of extraordinary, with a range of world-class restaurants offering diverse cuisines prepared by renowned chefs. The spa and wellness facilities provide a sanctuary for relaxation and rejuvenation, boasting an array of treatments and therapies tailored to individual needs. The rooftop pool and lounge area offer a tranquil oasis amidst the urban hustle and bustle, complete with cabanas and attentive staff catering to every whim.
